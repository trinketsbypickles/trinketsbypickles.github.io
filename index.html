About Trinkets by Pickles

Hi! I’m Pickles, the heart and hands behind every trinket you see here. I am an artist and interactive design graduate student who happens to be a big ecology nerd and a craft hobbyist. On the weekdays I work with reserved land and park spaces to create interactive learning tools that increase accessibility and engagement. On the weekends I create small-batch trinkets to sell! I create things like quirky earrings, stickers, buttons, and so much more.

All of my work is inspired by nature or video games. Small-batch making means I handcraft each piece. I don't use a manufacturer so I can reduce costs or deal with dead stock. I hope my trinkets can bring a little happiness into your day.

Thanks for stopping by and I hope to see you soon in person :)
